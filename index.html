Turks and Caicos Islands

Background Information about Turks and Caicos Island

Turks and Caicos Islands is located southeast of the Bahamas. The first inhabitants of the Turks and Caicos Islands were Tainos (known as "Lucayns").These settlers arrived around the year of 750 AD from other islands in the Carribeans. It consists of two groups of islands lying on the southeastern edge of the Bahamas. The Turks group is composed of Grand Turk Island, Salt Cay, and lesser cays. The Caicos group is located northwest of the Turks. Origin behind the name Turks and Ciacos - Turks comes from a refrence to the indigenous Turks head cactus and Caicos is from the Lucayan term "Caya Hico" meaning String of islands. Turks and Caicos is a beautiful site seeing island.
